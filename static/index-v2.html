Frequently asked

Questions we hear most often.

Is this a replacement for my vet?

No. crittr is triage and follow-through — a fast read on whether something needs the ER, a vet visit, or is safe to manage at home, plus the food, supplements and prescriptions that actually help. For in-person exams, labs, surgery and diagnostics, you still want your local vet. We'll tell you when that's the call.

How does the AI triage work?

You describe what you're seeing — species, age, symptoms, how long — and crittr asks a few follow-ups. Every symptom flow was written and reviewed by licensed vets. The output is one of three verdicts (ER now / Vet tomorrow / Safe at home) with the specific reason why. It's not a diagnosis; it's a well-informed gut-check so you don't panic-drive at 2am or miss something real.

Can I get a prescription here?

For our own-label products like the crittr heartworm chew and broad-spectrum topicals, yes — you start with a quick vet consult through one of our licensed partners, and if it's the right fit the Rx is written and shipped. We don't dispense prescriptions without a consult.

How fast does shipping ship?

Most supplements and OTC orders ship the next business day and arrive in 2–4 days. Rx items depend on the consult — typically 1–2 days to get the script written, then 2–4 days to arrive. Free shipping on orders over $40.

Is the first triage really free?

Yes. Triage is free, no account, no card. You only pay if you decide to consult a vet partner or buy something. We charge for products, not for answering questions.

What if my pet is having an emergency right now?

If your pet is collapsed, having trouble breathing, bleeding heavily, seizing, or otherwise clearly in crisis — skip the chat and go to your nearest emergency vet. crittr's job is to help you notice when something is serious. When it's clearly serious, get there first and read later.
